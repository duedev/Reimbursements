About

This app processes receipt photos locally on your machine, extracts vendor, date, and amount using OCR and a local AI model, and generates a polished Excel reimbursement report. No receipt data ever leaves your machine.

Getting Started
  1. Upload receipts using the "Add Receipts" card or drag and drop
  2. Wait for AI extraction to complete (watch the board update live)
  3. Review results — click any card to correct vendor, date, amount, or category
  4. Click "Generate Report" to export a polished Excel workbook

Pipeline Overview
  1. Auto-rotate — corrects photo orientation
  2. Grayscale + crop — cleans up and trims background
  3. OCR — built-in text recognition (RapidOCR)
  4. AI distillation — local LLM extracts vendor, date, amount
  5. Review — you verify and correct
  6. Export — polished Excel workbook with charts
Tips
  • Good lighting and a flat surface give better OCR results
  • You can edit any field in the review window before approving
  • The report includes charts and a category breakdown
  • All processing happens locally — nothing is sent to the cloud

Your report already embeds the receipt images, so the originals in the working folder are now just temporary. You can clear them to free space, or keep a copy in an archive folder — archived receipts are preserved and won't be flagged as orphaned. Either choice finishes this batch and clears the board.
